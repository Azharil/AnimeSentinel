[Database]
Streamers:
  {id, name, popularity, link_home, link_recent}

Shows:
  {id, mal_id, name, alts, description, latest_sub, latest_dub}

Streams:
  {streamer_id, show_id, anime_type, link}

Episodes:
  {anime_id, anime_type, episode_num, streamer_id, link, videolink, uploadtime, resolutions}

[Sentinel]
'Guard', executed by a cron job. Checks all streaming sites recently uploaded page.
'Pursue', searches all streaming sites to find all information for one anime. Used for adding new Anime.
'Inspect', searches one streaming site for all information on all anime. Used for adding a new Streaming Sites.
